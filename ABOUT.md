# About Umm Al Qura Calendar

The **Umm Al Qura Calendar** is the official Islamic (Hijri) calendar system
used in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for civil, administrative, and religious purposes.

It is adopted for determining Islamic months, Ramadan, Hajj, Eid holidays,
and official government records.

---

## What Is the Umm Al Qura Calendar?

The Umm Al Qura Calendar is a **calculated lunar calendar** based on
astronomical calculations performed for the geographical location of Mecca.

Unlike local visual moon sighting, this system allows Islamic dates
to be calculated and announced in advance with consistency and reliability.

---

## Official Use in Saudi Arabia

The Umm Al Qura Calendar is officially used by Saudi authorities for:

- Determining the beginning of Islamic months
- Announcing Ramadan and Eid dates
- Scheduling the Hajj season
- Government, legal, and financial records
- Public holidays and national planning

Because of this official role, the Umm Al Qura Calendar is widely referenced
both inside and outside Saudi Arabia.
